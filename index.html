ABOUT PET MATTER



Dr Rajat is an established veterinary practice in operation from many years. Pet Care Clinic is the best Dog and Cat Clinic In Jalandhar. We choose to constantly reinvent ourselves—by innovating and providing our clients with the most advanced veterinary care. We enjoy a clientele of long-standing friends and patients, but we also welcome new clients and patients every day.

We treat your dogs/Pets/puppies with great pampering and forever strive to put them away from any disease that might harm them in any way. Our dog clinic facility involves various medical measures that assure a diseases-free living for pets.

We know that one of the best things you can do to keep your pet healthy is to see that they get regular and attentive veterinary care. One of our clinic trademarks is access—we provide generous hours and easy access to veterinarians. Moreover, we are committed to attentive care. Most client communication is handled by veterinarians and is delivered in a timely fashion. You will not wait to hear from us, wondering and worrying. Our veterinarians return calls personally each day.
